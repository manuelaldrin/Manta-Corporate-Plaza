Manta Corporate Plaza

Developed by Manta Equities Inc, this is the newest Grade A addition near the Bonifacio Global City (BGC). Manta Corporate Plaza will be the upcoming premier office and commercial space in the next central business and lifestyle district, Arca South
A Tribute to

Filipino Architecture

Manta Corporate Plaza is a structure that will honor the Philippine cultural heritage. Designed by Archion Architects, it will be a modern adaptation of the “Bahay Na Bato.” It is structured to be a standout estate that will become the focal point of the township.

Green Booster For

Arca South

The structure gives a refreshing vibe to the city through its landscaped protruding balconies. These additions are embedded as stress-relieving spaces that will allow people to relax and take breaks.
